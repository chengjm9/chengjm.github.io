Looking for a Christ-Centered Community?

Welcome to Delight, a nation-wide women's ministry.

Welcome to Delight, we're glad you're here.

JMU's Delight chapter began in 2019 and has grown since! Being one of the 205 chapters, we've made an impact on our campus and the country. As of now, there are 14,760 college women involved in Delight.

Delight's Mission

Our mission is to invite college women into Christ-centered community that fosters vulnerability and transforms stories.

Our heartbeat is to provide invitational Christ-centered community on college campuses that lead women to fall deeper in love with Jesus. Delight chapters are recognized organizations, student-led, and rooted in Scripture-based curriculum and resources. We are passionate about empowering college women in the name of Jesus to lead, disciple, and transform the culture of their campus.
